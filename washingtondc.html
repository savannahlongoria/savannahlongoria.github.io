In the summer of 2017, I worked at the National Parks Conservation Headquarters in Washington, DC. Under the leadership of Ryan Valdez, the Senior Manager for Conservation Science & Policy, I helped increase public engagement through effective communication and compelling storytelling.

work

I created a customized interactive story map of all the National Parks across the country, specifically for the eleven NPCA regions across the country. This story map product is now used as the baseline for the communications work leading up to the NPCA Centennial in 2019.
